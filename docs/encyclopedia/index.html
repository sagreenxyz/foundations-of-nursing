A collaborative nursing knowledge wiki for nursing students and educators

Welcome to the NurseMastery Encyclopedia — an open-access, academic reference wiki covering foundational and advanced topics in nursing science. Articles are written at a formal, academic level appropriate for pre-licensure ADN and BSN students and are cross-linked to support self-directed, wiki-style learning.

Select an article from the sidebar, or browse by category below. Articles marked Available contain full academic text. Articles marked Coming Soon are planned for future sprints.
