## Q3: What content must Chapter 3 cover?

- Evidence-based practice (EBP): definition, the Iowa Model, ACE Star Model, PICO(T) framework
- Levels of evidence: systematic reviews, RCTs, cohort studies, expert opinion
- Research translation to bedside practice: barriers, facilitators, role of the bedside nurse
- Quality improvement (QI): PDSA cycle, Lean, Six Sigma, root-cause analysis
- National patient safety standards: Joint Commission NPSGs, The Leapfrog Group, IHI
- High-reliability organizations (HROs) and a culture of safety
- Nurse-sensitive quality indicators (NDNQi)
- Sentinel events, near misses, and incident reporting
- Human factors and error prevention: FMEA, checklists, bundles
- Interprofessional quality improvement teams
