## Part 1: What Does "Proceed with Chapter 4" Mean?

1. **What tasks remain for Chapter 4 after the first draft?**

   The master sprint backlog (`activity/2026-03-15-1210/sprint-backlog.md`) identifies two remaining tasks:
   - Developmental edit `[ ]` — reviewing the chapter for structural completeness, internal consistency, alignment with learning objectives, and editorial quality.
   - Copy edit / proofread `[ ]` — reviewing for grammar, punctuation, spelling, APA citation format, heading consistency, and key-term usage.

2. **Why break these into subtasks?**

   Both editorial passes involve distinct skills and distinct types of changes. By separating them, each pass can be completed and committed independently, making the changes reviewable and reversible. The developmental edit is performed first because structural changes (adding content, removing editorial artifacts) may create new copy-edit targets.
