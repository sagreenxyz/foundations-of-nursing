## Part 1: Why Break Into Components?

1. **What is the problem with drafting Chapter 4 as a single task?**

   Chapter 4 (*Therapeutic Communication*) is estimated at 45–55 pages and 10,000–12,500 words across nine sections (4.1–4.9), plus a case study, ten NCLEX questions, a summary, and a full reference list. Attempting to produce the entire chapter in a single work session creates:
   - Unacceptably long generation tasks prone to timeout or quality degradation
   - No intermediate checkpoints for review or course correction
   - Inability to parallelize review and drafting

2. **What is the component strategy?**

   Chapter 4 is divided into seven independently deliverable components. Each component represents a coherent subset of the chapter that can be drafted, reviewed, and committed on its own.
